Three years on and two cancelations later, this summer I have finally set off on my Kilimanjaro adventure!

This 6 day hike trekking up to the 'roof of Africa' has been the experience of a lifetime. I will keep very fond memories of the incredible guides and the group of fellow hikers on the trip, as well as of Tanzania as a whole!

I would like to thank everyone who donated to Hope for Children and made this trip! Speaking to the guides and porters your donations are greatly appreciated and go a long way to helping children and many Tanzanian families live happier and healthier lives.
