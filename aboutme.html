My name is Max Ryland and this is my first ever website.

I have built this website for my Data Science module as part of my Bsc in Economics and Politics at the University of Bristol.

Building this website has been an enjoyable yet challenging task which has taught me valuable skills.

I am a keen sportsman and football fan which is why I decided to base my project on the Economics of the the World Cup.

My project looks into the impact that major footballing events, such as the World Cup, has on stock markets.

Specifically, we look at the effects that particular matches as well as the World Cup as a whole have on individual stocks, on trading volumes, and on national indexes.
